## Section 2: Brand Voice & Visual Identity

**Tone:** Local · Competitive · Warm · Energetic

- **Local** — speak like someone from the colony, not a corporate brand. "Sunday morning cricket" not "weekend athletic sessions"
- **Competitive** — this is about winning streaks, leveling up, being the best in your building
- **Warm** — it's your crew, your ground, your game. Community first
- **Energetic** — short sentences. Punchy. Never boring

**Always do:**
- Start captions with a hook (question, bold statement, or "POV:")
- Use sport-specific emojis (🏏 🏸 ⚽ 💪)
- Tag the city (#Bangalore #Mumbai etc.)
- End with a clear CTA ("Link in bio" / "Comment your city" / "Tag your playing partner")
- Reply to every comment in the first hour

**Never do:**
- Use corporate language ("leverage synergies", "seamless experience")
- Post without hashtags
- Post blurry or dark videos
- Go more than 2 days without posting in the first 60 days
- Ignore DMs

**Visual direction:**
- Dark backgrounds (#0a0e1a navy) with bright sport accent colours
- Shot in natural light at actual turfs and grounds — authentic, not studio
- People mid-action (bat swings, shuttle hits, jump shots) — not posed
- Text overlays: bold white or indigo, minimal words, large font
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 for Reels and Stories, 1:1 for feed carousels

**Emoji usage:** 1–3 per caption line max. Never more than 5 in a row. Use sport emojis to break up text, not decorate it.
